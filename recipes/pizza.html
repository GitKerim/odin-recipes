Pizza

Pizza is a dish of Italian origin consisting of a usually round, flat base of leavened wheat-based dough topped with tomatoes, cheese, and often various other ingredients (such as anchovies, mushrooms, onions, olives, pineapple, meat, etc.).

A small pizza is sometimes called a pizzetta. A person who makes pizza is known as a pizzaiolo.

  • Meat
  • Mushrooms
  • Cheese
